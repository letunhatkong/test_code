Installation

To install download one of these packages and include the jquery.lighter.js and jquery.lighter.css files in your head with the following:

<script src="https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/2.0.2/jquery.min.js" type="text/javascript"></script>
<script src="javascript/jquery.lighter.js" type="text/javascript"></script>
<link href="stylesheets/jquery.lighter.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" />
